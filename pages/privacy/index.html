./privacy_check --verbose
OVERVIEW
Most websites track everything you do. They build profiles, sell your data, and participate in surveillance capitalism.
I don't. Why? Because I'm a privacy advocate who believes in FREEDOM. If I can't respect your privacy on my own website, why would you trust me with yours?
COLLECTION
NO_COOKIES — I don't use cookies. Not even "necessary" ones. This site works fine without them.
NO_ANALYTICS — No Google Analytics. No Hotjar. No heatmaps. No A/B testing. I have no idea how you got here or what you click on.
NO_THIRD_PARTY_SCRIPTS — Everything runs locally. No external JavaScript. No tracking pixels. No social media widgets that phone home.
YOUR_EMAIL — If you email me at connect@chrisvrakas.com, I store your email and message so I can respond. I don't add you to any marketing or mailing lists and I don't share with anyone.
SERVER_LOGS — I use Cloudflare as my CDN/DNS provider. Cloudflare keeps basic access logs (IP address, timestamp, pages visited, user agent) for security and DDoS protection. These logs are automatically purged after 72 hours. I don't access or analyze these logs unless investigating a security incident. The actual site content is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may keep internal logs that I cannot control or access.
I_DONT
✗ Sell your data
✗ Share your data with advertisers
✗ Build behavioral profiles
✗ Track you across websites
✗ Retarget you with ads
✗ Any surveillance capitalism bullshit
EXTERNAL_LINKS
I link to GitHub, Medium, X (Twitter), and other platforms. When you click those links, you're subject to their privacy policies, not mine. I can't control what they do.
YOUR_RIGHTS
Under GDPR, CCPA, and basic human decency, you have rights:
RIGHT_TO_KNOW: Ask what data I have
RIGHT_TO_DELETE: Request deletion of your data
RIGHT_TO_PORTABILITY: Get a copy of your data
RIGHT_TO_OPT_OUT: You are already opted out by default
Email me: connect@chrisvrakas.com — I'll respond within 48 hours.
ENCRYPTION
This site uses HTTPS (TLS 1.3). Your connection is encrypted. Your ISP can see you're visiting polymvthicpunks.com, but not which pages or what you're viewing.
For maximum privacy, I recommend considering the following options:
• Tor Browser
• VPN
• ProtonMail for encrypted email
• My PGP key for sensitive communications: View PGP Key
LEGAL_COMPLIANCE
I comply with:
• GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
• CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
• Common sense
LEGAL_REQUESTS
I may be legally required to comply with valid requests from law enforcement or government agencies. However, I can't share what I don't have — and I intentionally collect nothing about you.
My infrastructure:
• Domain: GoDaddy (registrar only)
• DNS/CDN: Cloudflare (keeps logs for 72 hours)
• Hosting: GitHub Pages (I don't control their logs)
If you need maximum privacy, use Tor Browser or a VPN. I can't see your real IP address if Cloudflare doesn't have it.
CHANGES_TO_THIS_POLICY
If I change this policy, I'll update the date below and note the changelog. Updates will be signed with my PGP key.
PHILOSOPHY
Privacy is a fundamental right. It isn't about having anything to hide — it's about having everything to protect.
You don't owe me your data just for visiting this website, and I don't need to know everything about you to do business together.
If more websites respected privacy by default, the internet would be a better place.
